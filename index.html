How to use
1
Save the receipt PDF from PioneerRx to the Dropbox folder on the PC. It will sync to the iPad automatically.
2
Tap "Load Receipt PDF" below and navigate to the Dropbox app → RxReceipts_Incoming folder → select the PDF.
3
Patient signs in the signature box with their finger.
4
Tap "Save & Download" — the signed receipt saves to your Photos and Downloads. Then attach it in PioneerRx.
